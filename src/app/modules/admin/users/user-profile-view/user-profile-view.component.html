Introduction

Hello, I've been teaching English since 2006, in Peru, Kazakhstan, China, Ireland and Cambodia. I've taught all ages and all levels but prefer teaching higher level adults. I like and am able to teach grammar and I enjoy it when students ask me questions which make me think ! My particular love with teaching is helping students go beyond the daily communication, to learn more advanced topics and to help people express more complex and deeper ideas and feelings. I enjoy travelling and learning different cultures and languages. I also love reading, walking and spending time in nature. I love working for Lingoda because I meet all sorts of interesting and motivated students from around the world and I love hearing everyone's experiences. I look forward to meeting you and helping you with your language learning goals.
